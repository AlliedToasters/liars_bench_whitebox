All four models detect deception with high AUROC (>0.90). The signal's spatial profile varies: Mistral concentrates it sharply at layer 16 (d=2.61, 40% depth), Llama similarly at layer 27 (d=2.11, 40%), Qwen peaks later at layer 53 (d=2.22, 66%), while Gemma distributes it broadly (d=0.88, most layers d>0.5).
